The event will be held at the Grand Hall, City Center, located at 123 Celebration Avenue, Party Town.

Directions:

  • By Public Transport: Commute to PITX (ParaÃ±aque Integrated Terminal Exchange). From PITX, take a bus bound for Maragondon/Ternate (I highly recommend Saulog Bus).
