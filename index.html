How to Get to the Event

The event will be held at the Function Events Hall at Paraiso de June Quijote Ecofarm.

Directions:

  • By Car: Use any digital maps application (e.g., Google Maps, Waze, Apple Maps) and search for "Paraiso de June Quijote Ecofarm" to navigate to the venue.
  • By Public Transport: Commute to PITX (ParaÃ±aque Integrated Terminal Exchange). From PITX, take a bus bound for Maragondon/Ternate (I highly recommend Saulog Bus). Inform the conductor that your stop is at Claros-Maragondon (Note: There are two Claros stops, one in Naic and one in Maragondon. Make sure to alight at the Maragondon stop). The route will follow the driving path: CAVITEX -> Kawit -> Tanza -> Naic -> Maragondon. Once in Maragondon, you will see the welcome arc. From the welcome arc, it is 1.9 km or approximately 3 minutes to your stop. When you see Petron and Claros on the left, and Alfamart on the right, you are in the correct location. At the tricycle terminal beside Alfamart, take a tricycle to the location. Inform the driver that you will alight at Don Quijote. Show them the picture. (Note: The location is not part of the tricycle's regular route, so this will be a special ride costing 100 pesos per trip. Each tricycle can accommodate 3-4 people.)
